Nagata Lab

My Role
Website Design
Tools
Photoshop, Sketch, Illustrator

NagataLab is a website for showcasing the professor’s research results on sustainable education and climate change education. This is a client project I collaborated with a company based in Japan during the pandemic. My role in this project is to redesign the website.

Target users are Including elementary, middle, and high school students, university students, language teachers, private sector stakeholders, ministry of the Environment office staff, and people interested in climate change.

Lunch Website

Challenges

・How to convey the ambiguous concept of “education” visually.・ Convey the message in visual“human, warm, accepting, diverse, Harmony between humankind and the environment”
・ Intergrate images that the professor likes such as sacred geometry, fibonacci series geometry, fractals.
・Present the original content more readability with a better information section design.

Process and Solutions

I proposed several different brand visuals and made colors variation for comparison.

Due to this app are for Japanese users. Japanese web font options aren’t many there as English. Japanese font usually has a large file size because it contains Chinese characters. It is important not to apply too many custom fonts for optimization and better rendering speed. I did my research on the Japanese system web fonts and adobe fonts for this design.

As a result, I applied two fonts only to have the best rendering performance. One for heading and one for body text.

Individual sections are visually separated by the use of different ui components, which form a uniform look because each component follows shared global rules.
